HTML Headings

Headings are important because search engines use the headings to index the structure and content of your web pages. Users often skim a page by its headings. It is important to use headings to show the document structure.
h1 headings should be used for main headings, followed by h2 headings, then the less important h3, and so on.
HTML headings are defined with h1 to h6 tags.
h1 defines the most important heading. h6 defines the least important
h1 is the biggest heading.
h6 is the smallest heading.
